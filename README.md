# Mini-CRM Rural
Vertical SaaS for operational management in family farming and small rural production.
________________________________________
## Overview
Mini-CRM Rural is a vertical SaaS initiative focused on supporting family farming operations and small rural producers with structured operational management tools.

The project begins intentionally simple. Its purpose is to validate whether a focused, low-complexity CRM tailored to rural workflows can improve commercial organization without increasing operational burden.

This repository also serves as part of a **public engineering journey**, where the system evolves incrementally from foundational implementation toward more mature production capabilities.
________________________________________
## Initial ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
The first validation focus targets:
•	Small rural producers (according to criteria to be defined in the future)
•	Family-run agricultural operations
•	Producers with limited digitalization
•	Businesses relying on informal records, spreadsheets, or manual tracking
•	Regional markets (initially Brazilian context)
This ICP will be refined through interviews and early validation cycles.
________________________________________
## Core Problem Hypothesis
Many small rural producers lack:
1.	Structured client tracking
2.	Organized sales history
3.	Visibility into recurring buyers
4.	Centralized communication records
As a result, operational data becomes fragmented and difficult to leverage for growth or professionalization.
Hypothesis:
A simple, vertical CRM tailored to rural workflows can reduce operational fragmentation and increase commercial clarity without introducing technological complexity.
________________________________________
## MVP Scope (Phase 1)
The initial MVP is intentionally limited and focused.
Expected core features:
•	Producer account management
•	Client registry (CRUD)
•	Transaction registry (CRUD)
•	Basic operational dashboard
•	Minimal role-based access
Not included in MVP:
•	Artificial intelligence
•	Marketplace functionality
•	External integrations
•	Distributed or microservices architecture
•	High infrastructure complexity
The objective is validation, not scale simulation.
________________________________________
## Roadmap Phases (High-Level)
- **Phase 1** — Core SaaS Validation
Build and validate operational CRM fundamentals.
- **Phase 2** — UX Refinement & Workflow Optimization
Improve usability and operational efficiency based on real feedback.
- **Phase 3** — Data Intelligence Layer (MercadoRaiz™ Evolution)
Introduce structured data insights when justified by real usage patterns.
- **Phase 4** — Conditional Strategic Integrations
Explore integrations only after proven traction and product-market clarity.
________________________________________
## Long-Term Vision
Mini-CRM Rural may evolve into a broader intelligent vertical SaaS platform under the ***MercadoRaiz™*** ecosystem.
Potential future capabilities (post-validation only):

- AI-assisted operational insights
- 	Structured data intelligence
- 	Matching logic based on validated use cases
- 	Carefully selected service integrations

No marketplace assumptions are embedded at this stage.
Expansion will only be considered after:
1.	Core SaaS validation
2.	Product-market clarity
3.	Measurable operational traction

In a more mature stage — **and only after strong SaaS validation** — the platform may explore partnerships or integrations that enable:

• Structured data generation to support access to rural financing and insurance
• Integration with remote sensing technologies
• Access to technical advisory services

The underlying intention of such integrations would be to democratize access to services and technologies that are currently difficult or expensive for small rural producers and family farming operations, contributing to increased competitiveness of this socio-economic segment.
